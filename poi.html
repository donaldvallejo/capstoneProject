Before you begin doing Poi you should check the total length of the cord. Hold the handle of the Poi and put your arm out. With the other hand hold onto the Poi. The Poi should only just reach the side of your arm pit.

Put the Poi in your hands and start swinging them in the direction shown. Take note that the palms of the hands are only facing down and forwards. Tips/Hints If you swing with your palms facing each other the Poi will come in together and cross or tangle. So always keep you palms facing down and forward. Pretend to push air away from you as the Poi pass over your head.

Good luck and happy spinning!
